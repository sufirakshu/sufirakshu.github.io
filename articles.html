Showing Up

March 6, 2019 : Wednesday

I was listening to the episode of Marie Forleo with Steven Pressfield today. I felt that I've been living like an amateur for wayyy too long and that it's high time to start living like a Pro. Marie went on to say that if we have a day job, we go reach there on time, do all the work required, clock out on time and then get home. And THAT, is the difference between an amateur and a professional.
So, in layman words, the difference between an amateur and a professional is how they show up to the work they do.

Today, I showed up, coded this 'Articles' page on the website and wrote my first article on this site. I sincerely hope I show up again tomorrow, and the day after that and the day after that, so that I can show myself that I'm not an amateur, but a professional. Aameen.
